How it works

Three steps. One minute. Dinner's handled.

01
🧠

AI plans your week

Every Sunday, OnlyPans generates a personalized meal plan based on your diet, budget, household size, and what you've eaten recently. No repeats, no waste.

02
🛒

Groceries, auto-matched

Ingredients are consolidated, deduplicated, and matched to real products at your preferred store. Pantry staples you already have are excluded.

03
📱

Tap, review, done

Get a notification with your meals and a one-click Instacart link. Swap anything you want, pick a delivery window, checkout. Groceries arrive in hours.

Instacart Integration

Single endpoint: POST /idp/v1/products/recipe creates a shoppable page with matched products. Users complete checkout natively in the Instacart app.

What We Don't Touch

Payment, delivery logistics, shopper assignment, and substitution handling are all managed by Instacart. We generate the list — they handle the rest.

Store Coverage

Instacart's network of 1,500+ retail banners means OnlyPans works everywhere — Whole Foods, Safeway, Costco, Kroger, and thousands more.

Human in the Loop

No order is placed without user review. Every week, you see the full meal plan and grocery list before tapping "checkout." Swap, remove, or add anything.
